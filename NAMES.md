# Brand Name Brainstorm

Goal: brandable, memorable, broad enough to cover **cats + dogs** across **health,
parasite, supplements & food**. Avoid regulated terms (**vet, clinic, pharmacy, Rx,
chemist**) — we're a content/affiliate site, not a provider. Prefer a clean `.co.nz`
(and ideally the matching `.com`).

> ⚠️ Verify availability before committing — check `.co.nz` at a NZ registrar
> (e.g. registrars listed on dnc.org.nz) and `.com` separately. Brand-conflict notes below.

## Shortlist

| Name | Why it works | Watch-outs |
|---|---|---|
| **PawWise** | "paw" + "wise" = trusted advice; short, brandable, covers all pets/topics | check PawWise isn't taken; common-ish |
| **PetWell NZ** | wellness angle; clean; broad (health + food + supplements) | generic-ish; "petwell" may exist overseas |
| **PawAdvisor** | advice/comparison positioning (like a "TripAdvisor for pet products") | longer |
| **ThrivingPets** / **PetThrive** | positive, premium, broad; good for supplements + food | check trademarks |
| **KiwiPaws** | strong NZ signal; friendly; memorable | NZ-only feel may cap global later (fine here) |
| **The Healthy Paw** | descriptive + brandable; SEO-friendly; warm tone | "Healthy Paws" = US pet **insurance** brand → use "The Healthy Paw" singular, verify |
| **PawPlan** | implies routine/recurring care (fits flea/worming schedules) | check availability |
| **PetCompass** | "guides you to the right product"; premium, broad | less obviously "pet health" |

## Recommended top 3
1. **PawWise** — best all-rounder: short, trustworthy, covers every territory, easy logo.
2. **PetWell NZ** — clean wellness brand; great for the supplements/food expansion.
3. **PawAdvisor** — strongest for the comparison/affiliate model specifically.

## Naming notes
- A descriptive `.co.nz` (e.g. brand + "nz") helps both trust and local SEO.
- Keep it pet-wide (not "flea"-specific) so the brand stretches across all four territories.
- Decide brand name before applying to affiliate programs (they ask for your site).

> `pet supplements` 70, `pet health` / `pet wellness` / `healthy pets` 10 each,
> `pet health nz` 0. So a keyword/exact-match domain will **not** drive search traffic
> (that's in the long-tail product queries). Pick a keyword-style name for **clarity &
