## Internal linking rules
- Every **spoke** links up to its territory **pillar** (exact-match or descriptive anchor).
- Every **pillar** links down to all its spokes, and across to 1–2 sibling pillars
  (e.g. Flea pillar ↔ Worming pillar via the combo spokes).
- The **"complete flea protocol"** and **flea-in-house** spokes cross-link treatment +
  home + comb + skin-relief products (multi-item basket = bigger commission).
- Q&A feeders link *into* the relevant money pillar, never the reverse as the primary CTA.
- Every page: affiliate disclosure + email-capture (re-treat reminder lead magnet).

## Monetisation per page
- **Affiliate** pages: Pet Direct (Linkshop) + Petstock (Commission Factory) buy links + comparison table.
- **Calming**: promote calming *products* (sprays, supplements, beds) — NOT prescription meds; refer to vet.
- **Food**: affiliate is low-margin — use for traffic + internal links into supplements, don't over-invest.
- **Q&A**: no direct money; capture email + funnel to money pillars.
